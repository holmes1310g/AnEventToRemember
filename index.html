About

We know your wedding day is one of the most important days of your life. At An Event To Remember, we love nothing more than working with brides to help them realize their wedding day dreams.

Whether you are planning a smaller, more intimate celebration or a larger, extravagant affair, we will work closely with you overseeing the placement of every detail. We begin by talking about your vision and reviewing your floral and decorating needs. We can go over pictures that are special to you and plan how we can incorporate them into your wedding day.

In the end, we create a beautiful and innovative look that is just “you!” We at An Event To Remember have been creating beautiful weddings for over 25 years and we are always keeping up on the latest wedding styles and upcoming trends. We can work within anyone’s budget. No wedding is too small or large to be beautiful.



Contact
